Stoichiometry Conversion


How this works

Enter the needed values for the amount, units, and what elements or molecules to convert from and to. Unfortunately, as of the present, it is only possible to convert the same elements or molecules to itself with different units, or from a molecule to an atom that it has. Converting from one molecule or atom to another is not yet possible since a balanced chemical equation is needed.

For exponents, kindly use the "^" symbol and encase the power with parentheses. Example: 10^(23), 6.022*10^(23)
